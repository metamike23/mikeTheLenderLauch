Loan Types

The conventional loan is generally accepted as the “gold standard” of residential lending because of its tighter guidelines. This loan is ideal for non-military with a 700+ credit score looking to get the absolute best rate possible. The minimum down payment for a purchase using a loan can also be as low as only 3% of the purchase price!

Loans backed by the Federal Housing Administration (FHA) are rising in popularity due to its somewhat looser qualifying guidelines. It allows those with credit scores as low as 580 to secure a mortgage. As well as allowing the minimum down payment for a purchase to be as low as only 3.5% of the purchase price.

Calling all military borrowers! If you’re looking to purchase any real estate the VA loan is the only reasonable option. Take advantage of all your VA benefits using this loan such as ZERO down payment on all purchases, no income documentation on interest rate reductions, and access to top tier rates!

Reverse mortgages can be a great option for the retired community to take advantage of your home's equity and get paid while doing it. Be aware you have to be 62 years or older in order to qualify for a reverse mortgage. If you would like to be paid a monthly check out of your home’s equity take advantage of a reverse mortgage today!

A home equity line of credit, more commonly known as a HELOC, is a great way to take advantage of your home’s equity. Think of it as a credit card tied to your home’s equity. However much equity you have you are entitled to use. However much you use that’s what you’ll owe and whatever you don’t use you don’t pay on.

Whether you want to tap into your home’s equity for a rainy day, an emergency, or just for some extra cash a cash-out refinance could be for you. Cash-out refinances are also very popular for those looking to renovate, repair, or even looking for funds to purchase their next property!

This loan is popular for self-employed borrowers because no income documentation is needed. All that is needed to do get pre-approved is 12-24 months of bank statements from whichever account shows the largest deposits, a valid form of ID, and a social security card. This loan can be used to purchase primary residences and / or investment properties.

Another loan popular amongst self-employed borrowers because no income documentation is needed. All that is needed to get pre-approved is one to two years of your business Profit and Loss (P&L) from your CPA, two months of business bank statements and a valid ID. This loan can only be used for purchasing a primary residence.

This loan is popular amongst investors because no income documentation is needed and it is asset driven but it can be used to purchase primary residence as well. In order to get pre-approved you must show a rental estimate proving the monthly rents can at least cover the monthly mortgage payment. You must also show two months of bank statements to show proof of down payment. The down payment can not be borrowed funds. Also a valid ID.
